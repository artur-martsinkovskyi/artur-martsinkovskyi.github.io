# Social network

Social network with extended media support. It also had a subscription management, various
statistics and video streaming. This project was developed from scratch to production and
maintenance stages.

Technologies:
- RoR
- PostgreSQL
- Bootstrap
- jQuery
- Lodash
- Lightgallery
- Omniauth
- Sproutvideo
- Cloudinary
- Google maps
